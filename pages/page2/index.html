Persona

Embark on a thrilling adventure as high school students uncover dark secrets in a mysterious world. A classic RPG with turn-based combat and a unique social simulation element.

Persona 2

A gripping tale where a group of teenagers confront a dangerous force connected to the power of rumors. This game features a rich narrative and deep character interactions.

Persona 3

Join a group of high school students who battle terrifying creatures in a mysterious tower during the dark hours of the night. This game introduces the iconic 'Social Link' system, blending dungeon crawling and social simulation.

A murder mystery unfolds in a rural town, where a group of friends must solve the case while battling dark forces from another world. Enhanced with new content, this version expands on the original's engaging story and character-driven gameplay.

Persona 5

As a group of rebellious teenagers, you infiltrate palaces within people's minds to change their hearts and save society. Stylish and bold, Persona 5 mixes dungeon exploration with a compelling story of resistance and friendship.
